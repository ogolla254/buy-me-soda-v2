Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 9, 2026

Welcome to Buy Me a Soda. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information Full name, username, email address, profile picture, creator bio, social media links, QR code links, public support page information
Transaction Information Payment amount, payment method, transaction date and time, supporter messages, technical information, IP address, device information
Analytics Data Cookies and analytics data for platform usage

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to create and maintain user accounts, process payments and donations, generate QR codes and creator pages, improve platform security, prevent fraud and abuse, provide customer support, and send important service updates.

3. Payment Processing

Buy Me a Soda does not directly store sensitive card information. Payments may be processed through trusted third-party payment providers such as PayPal, Stripe, GCash, or M-Pesa. These providers handle payment security according to their own privacy and security policies.

4. Public Information

Some creator information is publicly visible, including username, profile photo, creator bio, QR code page, and public supporter messages (if enabled). Users should avoid posting sensitive personal information publicly.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to remember user preferences and login status, analyze website usage and improve our services, detect suspicious activity, and provide personalized content and advertisements.
Users can disable cookies in their browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including secure servers, encrypted connections (HTTPS), access restrictions, and payment provider security protections. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.
We regularly update our security systems and limit employee access to your data.

7. User Rights

You have the right to access and update your personal information, request account deletion and data removal, contact support regarding privacy concerns, and opt out of marketing communications.

8. Children's Privacy

Buy Me a Soda is not intended for children under age 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or social media platforms. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of Buy Me a Soda after changes are posted means you accept of updated policies.
